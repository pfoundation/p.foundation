We firmly believe in the power and significance of open access to media and a free press. Recognizing their pivotal role in the success and development of nations and communities, we proudly present the MediaGuard program.

MediaGuard walks beside media organizations from all around the world, extending tailored support that helps them maintain a consistent and uninterrupted presence.

Depending on the specific needs of each organization, our aid encompasses a variety of services including backhaul support, downlinking, digital streaming, and content distribution.

The program is available for media organizations around the world. Organizations interested in taking part can apply through our [application page](/apply?program=mediaguard).
